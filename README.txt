Commands:

-add <name> <price> <role>
# Adds a normal role to the products
-addsub <name> <months> <price> <role>
# Adds a subscription to the bot

Add the role by tagging them: eg: -add Test 10 @Subscribers


-sendembed <name>

Sends an embed for a specific subscription to the role that was tagged

-buy <name>

Creates transaction flow for a transaction

Setup:
Pretty easy. Just open the config.json and change everything
